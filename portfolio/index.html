01 — About

A little about how I work.

I'm a BSIT graduate from Dalubhasaan ng Lungsod ng San Pablo, San Pablo City, Laguna — majoring in Web and Game Development. I'm drawn to full stack work specifically because I like owning a problem end to end: the schema, the logic that sits on top of it, and the screen someone finally taps on.

My most recent hands-on experience was an on-the-job training stint as a Web Developer at my university's Office of Student Affairs and Services, where I helped plan, build, and ship internal systems — alongside designing the posters and publication materials that announced them.

Outside of code, I care about the small, quiet details of a product — the kind that make something feel considered rather than assembled.
